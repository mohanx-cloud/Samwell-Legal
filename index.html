Samwell

A more thoughtful way to stay organized for taxes.
Samwell helps freelancers, independent contractors, and gig workers keep tax-related information in one place throughout the year. Track your overview, keep records organized, and return to tax season with more context already in place.

What it does

Samwell provides a structured way to keep track of your tax overview, expenses, and records over time. Instead of gathering everything at once, you can gradually build a clearer picture throughout the year.

Who it’s for

Designed for people with independent or flexible income — including freelancers, contractors, and gig workers — who want a simpler way to stay organized.
