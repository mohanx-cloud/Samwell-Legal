FAQ

Questions? We've got answers.

Everything you need to know before you get started.

Is Samwell free?

Yes. The core app is completely free. Take the survey, use your monthly checklist, and track receipts at no cost.

Is this a tax filing service?

No. Samwell is a tax organization tool, not a filing service. We help you track and organize your deductions year-round so filing is never a scramble.

Is my data safe?

Your data is encrypted and stored securely. Receipt images never leave your device. We do not sell your data or use it for advertising. Ever.

Who is Samwell for?

We built Samwell with freelancers, gig workers, and independent contractors in mind — Uber and Lyft drivers, DoorDash couriers, consultants, creatives, side hustlers. If you have income outside a regular paycheck and want to stay more organized at tax time, Samwell is for you.

How long does setup take?

About 2 minutes. Swipe through yes or no questions and your personalized deduction profile is ready instantly. No forms, no typing, just swipe.

Are the deduction estimates accurate?

All estimates are based on IRS-published rates and national averages. They are for organizational purposes only and do not constitute tax advice. Always consult a tax professional for your specific situation.
