3. Description of Service

Samwell is a personal tax organization tool designed to help freelancers, independent contractors, and gig workers track and organize information relevant to their annual tax filing. The App provides:

  • A personalized onboarding survey to identify potential deduction categories based on your work situation
  • Estimated deduction calculations based on IRS-published rates and national averages
  • A monthly checklist of tax organization tasks personalized to your deduction profile
  • A digital shoebox for logging receipt metadata (not images)
  • A tax overview showing estimated deductions and missed opportunities
  • A share summary feature for exporting a plain-text deduction summary
