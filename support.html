How We Can Help

Samwell is designed to help you stay organized, aware, and confident throughout the year. Taxes can feel overwhelming, especially when you’re managing freelance or independent income, but Samwell is built to make that process feel clearer and more manageable over time.

If something feels unclear, isn’t working as expected, or you simply have a question about how something works, you can always reach out. Whether it’s understanding a deduction, navigating your checklist, or reviewing your tracked expenses, we’re here to help you move forward with confidence.

Samwell is a tax organization tool, not a tax advisor or filing service. For personalized tax guidance, consult a qualified professional.

Support Topics

Account

Get help with signing in, resetting your password, updating your email, or restoring access to your account. If something doesn’t feel right with your login or account setup, we’ll help you get back in quickly.

Using Samwell

Questions about deduction estimates, monthly checklists, receipt logging, exports, or your tax overview are all welcome. If you’re unsure how something works or what something means, we’ll guide you through it clearly.

Feedback

If you’ve found a bug, noticed something that could be improved, or have an idea that would make Samwell better, we’d genuinely love to hear from you. Thoughtful feedback helps shape how the app evolves.
